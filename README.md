# 🚀 StudySpark AI

> **Transform your study material into an intelligent learning ecosystem.**

StudySpark AI is an AI-powered academic platform that helps students learn smarter—not harder. Upload your study material once and let AI automatically generate structured notes, adaptive flashcards, personalized quizzes, intelligent study plans, and exam readiness insights.

Built with a modern, premium **Liquid Glass-inspired interface**, StudySpark AI combines artificial intelligence, adaptive learning, and beautiful user experience into a single learning workspace.

---

## 🚧 Project Status

> **Hackathon MVP – Currently Under Active Development**

StudySpark AI is actively being built. Core features, AI integrations, and premium UI are under continuous development.

# 🌟 Vision

Modern students constantly switch between PDFs, notes, flashcards, AI chatbots, planners, and quiz platforms.

StudySpark AI unifies the complete learning workflow into one intelligent ecosystem.

Instead of managing multiple applications, students interact with a single AI-powered workspace that continuously adapts to their learning progress.

Generate structured, easy-to-understand study notes from uploaded documents.

- Key Concepts
- Definitions
- Formula Sheets
- Worked Examples
- Revision Checklist
- Interview Questions
- Practice Questions

## 🧠 Adaptive Flashcards

Automatically convert study material into intelligent flashcards.

- Spaced Repetition
- Confidence Tracking
- Difficulty Levels
- Smart Scheduling
- Personalized Revision

---

## ❓ Adaptive Quiz Engine

Generate quizzes based on:

- Uploaded Notes
- Weak Topics
- Previous Performance
- Exam Schedule
- Learning History

Supports:

- True / False
- Fill in the Blanks
- Short Answer
- Scenario-Based Questions

StudySpark AI follows a premium design language inspired by modern translucent operating systems while maintaining its own unique identity.

- Glass reflections
- Smooth scrolling

- Responsive layouts
